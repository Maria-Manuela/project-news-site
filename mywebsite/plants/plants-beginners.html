5 Plants That Refuse to Die (Even Under Your Watch)

The Snake Plant (Sansevieria): This plant is as tough as old boots. It doesn't care if you forget it exists for a month. In fact, it thrives on your neglect. Try to kill it? You’ll just be clutching at straws.

The Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum): This one loves to produce tiny baby plants. Before you know it, you will have a whole family. It grows so easily that you'll feel like a professional gardener without ever having to beat around the bush.

The ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia): This plant is like a ninja. It doesn't show up in your life until you least expect it. When you finally notice it, it will be thriving in a corner you forgot even existed. It's like it knows you need a reminder that plants can be mysterious.

Pothos (Devil's Ivy): This vine grows like crazy. Seriously, it spreads faster than gossip on a grapevine. If it starts to look sad, give it a splash of water, and it will come up smelling of roses in no time.

Cast Iron Plant (Aspidistra elatior): The name says it all. This plant is practically indestructible. You can completely ignore it, and it still won't kick the bucket. It is the absolute king of low-maintenance.
