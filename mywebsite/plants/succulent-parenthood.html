Plant Parenthood: Succulent Adopts Prickly Pear as Its Own

In a heartwarming balcony update, a tiny succulent has officially "adopted" a much larger prickly pear cactus as family.

They now share one pot, one sunny corner, and one watering routine. The succulent provides moral support. The cactus provides shade and confidence.

This is a good reminder for beginners: plants in the same pot should like similar light and water needs. Teamwork in a planter only works when everyone agrees on the basics.
