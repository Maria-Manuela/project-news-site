Topiary Tidbits: Hedgehog-Shaped Bush Sparks Adorable Trend

A small front-yard bush has gone viral after being trimmed into a hedgehog shape. Neighbors now stop daily to take photos and ask, "How do we get one?"

The gardener behind the trend says the secret is simple: pick a round bush, trim slowly, and imagine you are giving a haircut to a very patient woodland creature.

If you are a beginner, start with basic shapes before attempting full animal art. Even a slightly lopsided hedgehog still counts as garden charm.
