Botanical Buzz: Orchid Sets World Record for Most Elaborate Makeup

In a stunning display of natural glamour, a living-room orchid has won the unofficial title of "Most Elaborate Makeup" in plant history.

Judges (mostly the cat sitting nearby) praised its layered petals, soft pink tones, and dramatic center that says: "I woke up like this."

The orchid credits its success to bright indirect light, weekly admiration, and absolutely no watering schedule chaos. Beginner tip: treat your orchid like a celebrity — give it attention, but do not smother it.
