Drama in the Garden: Dandelion Accuses Lawn of Overcrowding

Breaking news from the backyard: one brave dandelion has filed a formal complaint against the lawn. The claim? "There is simply no room to bloom in peace."

Witnesses say the lawn responded with a classic line: "I was here first." The dandelion, unimpressed, spread seeds across three neighboring flower beds "by accident."

Garden experts recommend a truce: let the dandelion live, admire its yellow flower, and stop panicking every time it appears. Sometimes the loudest plant in the garden is also the most harmless.
